About dccode.gov

Welcome to dccode.gov. This site serves as the official home of the laws and resolutions of the Council of the District of Columbia. Once measures are passed by the Council, these laws and resolutions are compiled into the District of Columbia Statutes at Large and the District of Columbia Official Code.

Currently, this site only has the District of Columbia Statutes at Large for Council Period 20. In the coming months, more features will be added to the site, including the District of Columbia Official Code. If you have any questions about the site, please contact the Council of the District of Columbia, Office of General Counsel, at (202) 724-8026.
